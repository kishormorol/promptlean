Three variants. One job.

⚡ Lean

Minimum tokens, maximum leverage

For tight context windows, fast iteration, or when the model clearly understands the task. Gets you 80% of the result for 20% of the tokens.

⚖ Balanced

Structure without ceremony

The default choice. Enough context to guide output structure, not enough to waste your budget. Works across all major models.

★ Max Quality

Full instruction for the highest bar

When you need a research-grade summary, a thorough code review, or a rubric that holds up to expert scrutiny. Worth every token.
